My pet Komodo dragon

Reaching 3 meters in length and more than 135 kilograms, Komodo dragons are the largest lizards on Earth! It's a really great conversation starter and they scare away irritating people so win win. And plus we get along so well. We're both big cold blooded lizards he's basiclly my spirit animal.
